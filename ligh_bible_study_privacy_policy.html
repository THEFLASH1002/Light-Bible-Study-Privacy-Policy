LIGH✝ Bible Study

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026 • Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Ligh✝ Bible Study (“the App”) is an AI-powered Bible study companion built to help followers of Jesus Christ deepen their understanding of Scripture. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App or visit lightbible.study.

By using Ligh✝, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A password (stored in encrypted form — we never see it in plain text)

If you use the App as a guest without creating an account, we do not collect your name or email.

b. Bible Study Queries

When you use the Ask, Lookup, or guided study features, the questions and Scripture references you enter are sent to an AI service to generate responses. These queries may be processed and temporarily retained by our AI provider to generate your answer. We do not use your personal Bible study questions for marketing purposes.

c. Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid plan, your payment is processed by a third-party payment provider (such as Apple In-App Purchase or Stripe). We do not store your full credit card number or billing information on our servers. We receive confirmation of your subscription status only.

d. Usage Data

We may collect non-personally identifiable information about how you interact with the App, such as which features you use, session duration, and crash reports. This helps us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver AI-powered Bible study responses to your queries
  • Process your subscription and manage access to paid features
  • Improve the App’s features, performance, and user experience
  • Send you service-related communications (e.g., account confirmations, important updates)
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Guest Use

You may use limited features of Ligh✝ without creating an account. As a guest, we do not collect your name or email. Queries you submit as a guest may still be processed by our AI provider to generate responses.

4. Third-Party Services

Ligh✝ uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Gemini (AI responses)
  • API.Bible / American Bible Society (Scripture data)
  • Supabase
  • OpenRouter

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services. We are not responsible for their data practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial record-keeping purposes.

Bible study queries are not permanently linked to your account for the purpose of building behavioral profiles.

6. Children’s Privacy

Ligh✝ is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption of passwords and secure transmission of data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notice. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
